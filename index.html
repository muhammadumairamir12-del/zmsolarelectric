About ZM Solar & Electric

Our Story

Powering Pakistan With Clean Energy Since 2014

ZM Solar & Electric was established with a single mission: to make reliable and affordable solar energy accessible to every Pakistani household and business. Starting from Lahore, we have now served over 1,200+ satisfied customers across Punjab.

We’ve earned a strong reputation for reliability, high-quality installations, and long-term energy savings. Whether you’re a homeowner looking to lower your electricity costs, a business aiming to reduce your carbon footprint, or a property developer integrating green energy, we provide solar systems that are custom-designed, energy-efficient, and built to last. We are authorized dealers of top brands including Jinko Solar, LONGi, Canadian Solar, Growatt, Volta Batteries, and many more — ensuring genuine, certified products with full manufacturer warranties.
